# 💫 About Me:
I build AI systems that make pharmaceutical operations faster, smarter, and less manual.<br><br>

As an AI Engineer at Sthree Chemicals, I develop Generative AI and RAG-based applications that let business and sales teams query internal pharma documents, API product data, and customer information in plain language — turning hours of manual lookup into instant, context-aware answers.<br><br>

My edge is the combination of two worlds most people keep separate: applied AI and real pharmaceutical operations. Before moving fully into AI, I ran production planning and built automation at Unichem Laboratories, where I cut the production planning cycle from 4–5 days to under 3 hours and automated SAP-integrated reporting workflows. I know what the data actually means on the factory floor — which makes the AI I build genuinely useful, not just technically impressive.<br><br>

### What I Work With
- Generative AI, LLM Integration, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Prompt Engineering, Vector Databases, NLP
- Python, SQL, REST APIs, React, Node.js for Full-Stack AI Applications
- SAP, VBA, Dashboard & Workflow Automation

Open to connecting with people working at the intersection of AI and industry. If you're solving hard problems with LLMs in manufacturing, supply chain, or pharma, let's talk.
