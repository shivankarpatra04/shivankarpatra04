# 💫 About Me:
I build AI systems that make pharmaceutical operations faster, smarter, and less manual.<br> <br>As an AI Engineer at Sthree Chemicals, I develop Generative AI and RAG-based applications that let business and sales teams query internal pharma documents, API product data, and customer information in plain language — turning hours of manual lookup into instant, context-aware answers.<br> <br>My edge is the combination of two worlds most people keep separate: applied AI and real pharmaceutical operations. Before moving fully into AI, I ran production planning and built automation at Unichem Laboratories, where I cut the production planning cycle from 4–5 days to under 3 hours and automated SAP-integrated reporting workflows. I know what the data actually means on the factory floor — which makes the AI I build genuinely useful, not just technically impressive.<br> <br>What I work with: • Generative AI, LLM integration, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) • Prompt engineering, vector databases, NLP • Python, SQL, REST APIs, React/Node.js for full-stack AI apps • SAP, VBA, dashboard & workflow automation<br> <br>Outside client work I build side projects to push my skills — an ML-powered content analysis tool (Gemini + BART), a job-application analytics tracker, and an AI counseling platform.<br> <br>Open to connecting with people working at the intersection of AI and industry. If you're solving hard problems with LLMs in manufacturing, supply chain, or pharma, let's talk.
